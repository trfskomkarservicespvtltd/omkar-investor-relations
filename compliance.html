Compliance & Legal Framework

Ensuring transparency, accountability, and adherence to all applicable laws & MOA objects.

Our Legal Framework

Omkar Enterprises operates in strict compliance with the Companies Act, 2013, and all transactions are aligned with the Memorandum of Association (MOA) of TRFSK Omkar Services Pvt Ltd.

  • All investor agreements are executed on valid stamp paper.
  • KYC norms are strictly followed for every investor.
  • TDS and tax obligations are duly complied with.
  • All business activities are limited to MOA-approved objects.

Risk Disclosures & Disclaimers

Important: All investments carry inherent risk. Past performance should not be considered a guarantee of future returns.

  • Omkar Enterprises does not promise fixed guaranteed returns unless specifically documented in agreements.
  • All investment activities are subject to business, market, and regulatory risks.
  • Investors are advised to read agreements carefully before investing.
  • Any early withdrawals may be subject to terms and conditions.
  • We comply with all MOA restrictions; no business is done outside our approved scope.

Compliance Policies

AML & KYC Policy

All investors must submit identity & address proofs to comply with Anti-Money Laundering guidelines.

Tax Compliance

We deduct and deposit applicable TDS, and issue Form 16A or other certificates to all investors.

Audit & Reporting

Annual accounts are audited, and investor statements are prepared quarterly/annually.

Dispute Resolution

All disputes are subject to arbitration and Indian jurisdiction as per agreement terms.
